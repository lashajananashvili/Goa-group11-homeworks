Shower Process Steps

Step 1: Turn on the water and adjust it to your desired temperature. Wait until the water is comfortably warm before stepping in.

Step 2: Wet your entire body thoroughly. Make sure your hair is completely soaked to prepare for shampooing.

Step 3: Apply a quarter-sized amount of shampoo to your scalp. Massage it in with your fingertips to create a rich lather.

Step 4: Rinse the shampoo out of your hair. Use your fingers to help remove all traces of shampoo, leaving your hair clean and fresh.

Step 5: Apply conditioner, focusing on the ends of your hair. Let it sit for a few minutes before rinsing it out with cool water for added shine.
